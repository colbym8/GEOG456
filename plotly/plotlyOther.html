This bar graph with no height aspect, but demonstrates changes in the data using a color scale exclusively is commonly seen in
visualizations showing temperature change, many of which are mean global temperature used to demonstrate climate change's effects.
The darker red hues indicate higher levels of water consumption, with the blue hues representing lower levels resptively. I decided
to leave the x-axis information present to show the time period of the data, with each bar representing one year's global water
consumption.
